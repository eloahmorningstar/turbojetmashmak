“Turbo Jet Covert Ops” is commonly used by players to describe a fast farming strategy within the Covert Ops mode. In this mode, players enter a PvE mission where they fight AI-controlled enemies, collect resources, and extract safely without competing against other player squads. The term “Turbo Jet” refers to using a mech’s boost or jet propulsion to quickly rush toward high-value areas of the map—often the center—where elite enemies and better loot are more likely to spawn. By moving at maximum speed and engaging stronger targets immediately, players can gather materials, upgrades, and rewards much faster than by exploring the map slowly. As a result, “Turbo Jet Covert Ops” generally means completing quick, efficient runs in Covert Ops by aggressively boosting across the map to farm valuable loot and then extracting once the objective is achieved.
